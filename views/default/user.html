Happy Goblin

Happy Goblin is a tool to track the game stats in your playgroup. Life counters included! It's mobile friendly and tracks your life (and death) on the go!

Is Edric really giving you the advantage in Commander? Does the Red Deck Win in modern? Try Happy Goblin and find out!
